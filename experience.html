My Experiences

During my semester break, as part of the academic requirements for Semester 5, I completed a practical training internship at Majlis Bandaraya Iskandar Puteri (MBIP). This internship gave me important hands-on experience in a real-world working setting while also allowing me to utilize the academic information I obtained from my Information Management courses.

Throughout the internship, I was assigned a variety of activities that had a connection to my subject of study, such as opening new files, entering data into Microsoft Excel, and making cybersecurity awareness posters. This internship helped me improve both my technical and soft abilities. It was a pleasant experience that helped me better grasp how IT and information management are integrated in municipal government settings.
